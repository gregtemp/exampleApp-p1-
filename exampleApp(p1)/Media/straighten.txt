The straighten filter rotates the source image by the specified angle in radians.
[intro]
Call the straighten method from the source image (image you want to affect). 
The image is scaled and cropped so that the rotated image fits the extent of the source image.
 
The angular value to calculate the adjustment, defaults to 0.0, minimum -PI, maximum PI.
[body]
[image straighten:-PI/2];
[code]
